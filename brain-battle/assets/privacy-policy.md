# Privacy Policy — Brain Battle

**Last updated: 2026-03-26**

## 1. Overview

Brain Battle ("the App") is a two-player cognitive training game designed for use on a single shared device. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

## 2. Data We Collect

The App collects only the data you directly provide:

- **Player names** — entered by users before each game session.
- **Game scores and accuracy** — recorded locally after each game round.
- **Session history** — timestamps and per-game results stored on-device.

The App does **not** collect:

- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Contacts, photos, or any other system data
- Analytics or crash telemetry sent to external servers

## 3. How Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database (via expo-sqlite). No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or otherwise disclose any user data to third parties. The App has no internet permission and makes no network requests.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

All data persists on-device until you uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored data.

If you wish to clear game history without uninstalling, you may clear the App's storage via your device's Settings → Apps → Brain Battle → Clear Data.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Brain Battle is suitable for users of all ages. The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond the player name typed at game start, which is stored locally and never transmitted.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

## 8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
